Chicken Wing Dippers

""

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken wings, wing tips removed and wings split into two pieces
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sunflower oil

Instructions

  • Put the wings in a large bowl and toss with 1 tsp salt, the cornflour and baking powder - this will help the skin go crispy when baking. Chill, covered, for at leat 1 hr, or ideally overnight.
  • Heat the oven to 220℃ / 200℃ fan / gas 7. Toss the wings with the oil, then arrange in a single layer on a wire rack set over a baking tray. Bake for 25-30 mins until golden, then turn the wings over and bake for 25-30 mins more until crisp. Transfer to a platter with sauces for dipping, or put a large bowl on the table next to each sauce and toss the wings in the sauces.
