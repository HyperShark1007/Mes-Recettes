Aperol Spritz

"Enjoy a taste of summer with this classic Italian cocktail, the Aperol spritz. Mix Aperol, prosecco, and soda for a refreshing drink."

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 100ml Aperol
  • 150ml prosecco
  • soda, to top up

Instructions

  • Put a couple of cubes of ice into 2 glasses and add 50 ml measure of Aperol to each. Divide the Prosecco between the glasses and then top up with soda, if you like.
